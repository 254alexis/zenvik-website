ORBIT POSITION

The orbit must follow the outer edge of the entire service card.

Do not orbit only the illustration area.

Do not place the orbit in the center of the card.

The orbit path must match the card shape.

Requirements:

* Rounded rectangle path
* Same border-radius as the card
* Positioned around the card edge
* Slightly outside the card edge by 4px–8px
* Must not cover card content or illustration

* 1.06 to 1.08

ORBIT LINE + BEAM COLORS

Base orbit trail:

Moving beam head:

ORBIT TRAIL

The base orbit trail must be very subtle.

Requirements:

* Thin line
* 1px to 1.5px
* Low opacity
* Color: #7a6200

==================================================

MOVING BEAM

The moving beam must be the visible active part.

Requirements:

* Color: #dfa408
* Length: 18%–25% of the orbit path
* Beam head brighter than tail
* Tail fades gradually into transparent
* Beam completes one revolution
* Beam fades out after completing the revolution

==================================================

ANIMATION SPEED

One full orbit revolution should take:

2.2 to 2.8 seconds

Do not make it fast.

Do not make it feel like a loading spinner.

==================================================

COMPLETION BEHAVIOR

After one revolution:

* Beam fades out smoothly
* Base trail fades out smoothly
* Card returns to normal scale
* Move to the next card after 0.25–0.4 seconds

VISUAL GOAL

The effect must feel like a premium scanning spotlight moving around the selected service card.
