SPOTLIGHT SEQUENCE

The spotlight activates one service card at a time.

Current card becomes the temporary focal point.

After completion:

Return card to normal state.

Move to the next service card.

==================================================

CARD ACTIVATION

When a card becomes active:

Scale:

* 103% to 105% maximum

Glow:

* Slight increase only
* Maintain premium appearance

Illustration:

* Slight brightness increase
* Existing service animations remain active

Duration:

0.8 to 1.2 seconds

==================================================

ORBIT RING

When a card becomes active:

Create one orbit ring around the illustration area only.

Requirements:

* Not around the entire card
* Surround illustration section only
* Gold color (#dfa408)
* Stroke width: 2px–3px

Ring Style:

* Partial orbit ring
* Approximately 70%–80% of a full circle
* Small gap remaining

Do not create:

* Loading spinners
* Complete circular borders
* Dashboard loaders

==================================================

ORBIT MOTION

The orbit ring performs:

* One smooth revolution around the illustration
* Controlled speed
* Premium movement

After completing one revolution:

* Ring fades out
* Card returns to normal

Then move to the next card.
