# Zenvik Technologies Website Structure

## Main Website: `zenviktechnologies.com`
The main website is the public marketing and sales property.

It owns:
- Service discovery
- Hosting and cloud marketing
- Domain and SSL marketing
- Software development
- Website and ecommerce services
- Marketing and branding
- ICT and networking services
- AI services
- Blog
- FAQs
- Legal pages
- Pricing pages
- Contact and quote flows

## Homepage Structure Guidance
The homepage should not be a plain list of every service or a hosting-plan sales page.

Recommended ingredients:
- Header and navigation
- Hero with broad technology positioning
- Trust and capability signals
- Cross-marketing service stories
- Selected service highlights
- Hosting/cloud pathway without overpowering the page
- Software, websites, marketing, ICT, and AI pathways
- Process or delivery confidence
- Real portfolio or honest placeholders
- FAQs or buyer guidance
- Final CTA separated from the footer
- Footer

## Portal Website: `portal.zenviktechnologies.com`
The portal is the WHMCS customer management system.

It owns:
- Login
- Registration
- Customer accounts
- Invoices
- Tickets
- Checkout
- Support
- Customer care
- Client area workflows

The portal homepage must not become a hosting sales homepage, pricing hub, full service marketing page, or domain search landing page. It may lightly guide users back to the main website for service discovery.

## Shared Design Requirements
- Same logo
- Same brand colors
- Related typography feel
- Related button language
- Related spacing and card style
- Trust-focused tone
- Main-site remains the design authority
- WHMCS portal remains the customer workflow authority
