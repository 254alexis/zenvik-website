# Zenvik Technologies Brand Guide

## Brand Role
Zenvik Technologies must feel like one connected ecosystem across:
- `zenviktechnologies.com`: the main marketing, sales, and service discovery website.
- `portal.zenviktechnologies.com`: the WHMCS customer portal for account and support workflows.

The main-site is the visual and design authority. Portal styling should inherit the same brand language while respecting WHMCS functionality and constraints.

## Primary Colors
- Primary Blue: `#043a7e`
- Gold Accent: `#7a6200`
- CTA Orange: `#dfa408`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Light Gray: `#F8FAFC`
- Dark Text: `#0F172A`

Use Zenvik brand colors only unless a local accessibility or system-state need requires a standard semantic color.

## Brand Feel
- Premium
- Trustworthy
- Enterprise-grade
- Modern technology-focused
- Clean and professional
- Helpful without becoming casual or cluttered

## Visual Direction
- Strong blue should anchor high-confidence sections, headers, and primary CTAs.
- White and light-gray sections should create calm reading space.
- Gold and orange accents should be used sparingly for emphasis, highlights, and conversion moments.
- Portal pages should look related to the main-site, but they should remain practical customer-care screens rather than marketing pages.

## Portal Brand Constraints
- Custom portal CSS classes must use the `zt-` prefix.
- Do not replace or upgrade WHMCS framework dependencies just to match the brand.
- Do not remove WHMCS variables, scripts, includes, hooks, overlays, or functional markup while applying brand styling.
